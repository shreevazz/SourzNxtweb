What sets us apart

Built different, by design.

SourzNxt combines auction depth, AI intelligence, and verified networks into a single transparent platform — purpose-built for India's industrial commerce.

100% transparent transactions ▾

Eliminate backdoor deals and information gaps. Every quote submission, negotiation turn, and final award counter-step is logged transparently to ensure absolute integrity across your purchasing pipeline.

Unified multi-plant dashboard ▾

Consolidate independent factory operations into a centralized command view. Monitor live allocation runs, scrap metrics, and procurement workflows across your entire industrial network seamlessly.

AI-powered intelligence (ZIT) ▾

Deploy zero-information trading agents to automate complex multi-party offers. Smart analytics constantly predict optimal pricing boundaries without leaking structural margin targets to third parties.

Verified vendor network ▾

Gain immediate access to pre-vetted enterprise suppliers and MSME vendors. Bypassing unverified brokers ensures quality control compliance, timely logistics matching, and secure downstream execution.

Seamless ERP integration ▾

Bridge auction workflows directly into your legacy infrastructure. Sync vendor master files, ledger logs, RFQ lines, and cleared transactional assets without manual data entry friction.

Full lifecycle coverage ▾

Manage material pipelines smoothly from initialization to processing closure. Oversee forward scrap disposal lines, reverse procurement steps, and strategic compliance auditing inside one console.
